About SentinX

AI-Powered Cyber Threat Prevention

SentinX is an AI‑driven cybersecurity platform that turns three independent business systems into live data sources feeding one central security brain.

Our Vision

Security that understands context, not just logs

Smarter monitoring

We aim to give security teams a single place to see what is happening across banking, travel, and healthcare, instead of jumping between separate tools.

AI at the core

The platform is designed around AI models that classify behavior and detect anomalies, helping teams focus on the incidents that truly matter.

Realistic simulation

By modeling three different industries, SentinX mirrors how modern organizations really operate: many heterogeneous systems, one security operations center.

Why This Is a Strong Graduation Project

Beyond simple CRUD apps

Three real domains

You did not just build demo forms; you simulated banking, airline booking, and healthcare systems with meaningful business logic and real‑world flows.

Embedded security

Each system has its own security controls, rate limiting, validation, and logging, turning every app into a rich security telemetry producer.

Central AI platform

The separate systems feed a centralized platform that aggregates logs, runs machine learning models, and coordinates alerts and blocking decisions.

SOC-style operations

SentinX mimics how a real SOC works: events come in, AI classifies them, analysts receive alerts, and automated rules can block malicious behavior.

Who We Serve

Organizations that need clear visibility into modern threats
