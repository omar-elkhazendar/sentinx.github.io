Security Dashboard

The central platform that turns logs into actionable security insight

How It Works

Bank, Airline, and Medical systems push events to the Dashboard. AI models classify them, and the platform drives alerts and blocking.

Data Ingestion

HTTP APIs receive logs and attack reports. Events are validated, normalized, and stored in a unified timeline.

AI Classification

ML models classify events: normal vs. attack, with categories (brute-force, SQLi, XSS, etc.) and risk scores.

Visualization

Attack dashboards, severity filters, per-system charts, top source IPs, and AI model statistics.

Response Actions

Alerts, IP blocking, auto-block rules, and feedback loops for continuous improvement.
